### Key Features - Most important technical specifications

- **Capacity:** Capacities range from small 3-cup choppers to large 14-cup models. A 8-10 cup processor is a versatile sweet spot for most families.
- **Blades and Attachments:** At a minimum, a food processor should come with a multi-purpose S-blade for chopping and pureeing. Many models also include discs for slicing and shredding. A dough blade is a must-have for bakers.
- **Controls:** Look for simple, intuitive controls. A basic "Pulse" button is crucial for controlling texture. Some models offer multiple speeds or even preset programs for specific tasks.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Even on a budget, you can find well-built machines. Look for models with a heavy, stable base that won't vibrate across the counter during use.
