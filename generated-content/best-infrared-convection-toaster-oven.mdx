## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Infrared Convection Toaster Oven

Choosing the right infrared convection toaster oven involves more than just picking the one that looks best. Consider these key factors to ensure you invest in an appliance that perfectly suits your cooking style and kitchen space.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you buy, think about what you'll cook most often. Are you a family of four that needs to roast a whole chicken, or are you a single person who mostly toasts bagels and reheats pizza? Your primary use will determine the ideal capacity. A 6-slice or 26-quart oven is great for families, while a smaller 21-quart model might be perfect for a couple or a small apartment. Also, consider the types of functions you need. If you love making jerky or dried fruit, a dehydrate function is a must. If you're an avid baker, look for precise temperature controls.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Infrared convection toaster ovens can range in price from around $150 to over $400. Entry-level models will offer basic functions and solid performance.
