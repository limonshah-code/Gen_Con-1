## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Electric Roaster For Turkey

Choosing the right electric roaster can elevate your holiday cooking from stressful to seamless. To make an informed decision, consider these key factors that separate a good roaster from a great one.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you buy, think about how you'll use the roaster. The primary consideration is size. How large is the typical turkey or roast you cook? Roasters come in various quart capacities, which translate to the maximum weight of poultry they can hold. A 24-quart roaster can typically handle a 28-pound turkey, while a 28-quart model can fit a 30-pounder. Also, consider versatility. Do you want a unit that can also bake, steam, or slow cook?
