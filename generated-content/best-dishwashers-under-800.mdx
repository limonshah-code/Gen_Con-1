## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Dishwashers Under 800

Choosing the right dishwasher involves more than just picking the one that looks best. Here are the key factors to consider to ensure you get the perfect appliance for your home.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, determine what type of dishwasher you need. Do you have space for a full-size, 24-inch built-in model like the Bosch 800 Series, or is your kitchen better suited for a portable countertop unit? Consider your living situation. If you are a renter or live in an RV, a countertop model with a built-in water tank like the SHIPUNAYE offers incredible flexibility. For homeowners, a built-in unit provides a more permanent and integrated solution. Also, think about your household size. A single person may only need a small compact unit, while a small family will benefit from a larger capacity countertop or built-in model.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
While all the dishwashers in this guide are under $800, there is still a significant price range. Entry-level countertop models can be found for around $250-$400, offering basic but effective cleaning. Mid-range countertop units with more features like better drying or larger capacity typically fall between $400-$600. High-end built-in models like the Bosch can approach the $800 mark. Set a realistic budget based on the features that are most important to you, whether that's portability, capacity, smart features, or quiet operation.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look for features that will make your life easier. Multiple wash cycles (e.g., heavy, normal, quick) are essential for handling different types of loads. A stainless steel tub is more durable, quieter, and dries better than a plastic one. For countertop models, check the water connection type—some connect to a faucet, while others have a built-in tank. Advanced features like a soil sensor, which adjusts the cycle time based on how dirty the dishes are, or a hybrid drying system can add significant convenience.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
A dishwasher is an investment, so you want it to last. Look for models made with high-quality materials like stainless steel, both for the exterior and the interior tub. Check for sturdy racks that slide smoothly and feel robust. Reading user reviews can provide insight into the long-term reliability of a particular model. Brands with a reputation for quality, like Bosch, are often a safe bet, but many newer brands also offer excellent, durable products.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
While not the only factor, brand reputation can be a good indicator of quality, customer service, and warranty support. Established brands like Bosch have a long history of producing reliable appliances. However, do not discount lesser-known brands, especially in the countertop market. Many, like those featured in this guide, specialize in compact appliances and offer innovative features and competitive quality. Always check the warranty offered, as it shows the manufacturer's confidence in their product.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
Measure your available space carefully before buying. For built-in models, you will need a standard 24-inch wide opening. For countertop models, measure the height, width, and depth of your counter space, paying attention to the clearance under your cabinets, especially for models with flip-up lids.
