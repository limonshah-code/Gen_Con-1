Furthermore, consider air quality. If you have allergies, pets, or live in a polluted area, a model with a HEPA purifier is essential. For specific concerns like formaldehyde or NO₂, a premium model like the HP2 De-NOx is the best choice.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($300-$800 ranges)
Dyson products come at a premium, so it is important to set a realistic budget. Cooling-only fans like the AM07 typically start in the $300-$400 range. Models that add purification, like the TP10, are often in the $400-$550 range. For the all-in-one Hot+Cool+Purifier models, expect to pay anywhere from $550 to over $800 for the most advanced versions. Don't forget to consider the cost of replacement filters, which are typically needed every 12 months and can cost between $60 and $80.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Pay close attention to the technical specifications. **Oscillation range** is key for room coverage; models with 350° oscillation are better for large, open spaces, while 70° may suffice for personal use. **Smart connectivity** via the MyDyson app offers convenience and detailed reporting, but if you prefer simplicity, a remote-only model like the TP10 is a great option. Look at the **number of airflow settings** for precise control. Finally, if you are a light sleeper, check for a dedicated **Night Mode**, which ensures quiet operation and a dimmed display.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Dyson is renowned for its build quality and innovative engineering. The products are typically constructed from high-quality ABS polycarbonate, the same material used in riot shields and crash helmets, ensuring durability. The bladeless design not only enhances safety but also means there are fewer moving parts to break. Look for certifications like the Asthma and Allergy Foundation of America (AAFA) seal on purifier models, which validates their effectiveness and quality.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
With a brand like Dyson, you are investing in a reputation for innovation, performance, and customer support. James Dyson and his team of engineers are known for relentlessly testing and refining their products. This commitment to research and development results in machines that are not only effective but also energy-efficient and built to last. While the initial cost is high, the reliability and long-term performance often justify the investment compared to less-established brands.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
Consider the physical footprint of the fan. The tower fans (TP and AM series) have a slim, vertical design that takes up minimal floor space, making them ideal for apartments or smaller rooms. The desktop or personal models (like some configurations of the AM09) are more compact and easily moved. Check the dimensions and weight of the model you are considering to ensure it will fit comfortably in your intended space and can be stored easily during off-seasons if necessary.
