## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Microwave Cover For Food

Choosing the right microwave cover can significantly improve your cooking experience. Here are the key factors to consider to ensure you select a product that meets your needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, consider the size of your microwave and the dishes you use most often. Measure the interior height and width of your microwave and the diameter of your largest plates. A cover should be large enough to fit over your food without touching it, allowing steam to circulate. If you have a small kitchen, a collapsible cover like the Tovolo model can be a great space-saver. For those who prioritize safety, a glass cover or one that comes with a mat for handling hot dishes might be the best fit.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Microwave covers are generally affordable, with prices ranging from about $10 to $30. Basic plastic models, like the Nordic Ware set, are typically in the $10-$15 range and offer excellent value. Mid-range options, often featuring silicone or collapsible designs, fall between $15 and $25. Premium glass covers or innovative magnetic designs can cost upwards of $25. Set a budget based on the features you value most, whether it's material, storage, or multi-functionality.
