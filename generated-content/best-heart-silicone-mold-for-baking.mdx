Consider the mold's structure. Standard silicone molds are very flexible, which is great for unmolding but can lead to spills when moving batter. **4. Check Temperature Ratings:**
Verify the mold's temperature range. A good mold should be safe for a wide range of temperatures, typically from around -40°F to 450°F. This ensures it can go from the freezer to the oven without any issues, maximizing its versatility.
