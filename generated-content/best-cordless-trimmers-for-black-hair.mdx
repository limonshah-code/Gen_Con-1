### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
- **Motor:** Look for a powerful rotary or magnetic motor. Rotary motors are versatile and strong, ideal for cutting through thick, coarse hair. Speeds of 6,000-7,200 SPM (Strokes Per Minute) are a good benchmark.
- **Blades:** High-carbon steel or titanium blades are durable and stay sharp longer. The ability to zero-gap the blade is a critical feature for close cuts and sharp lines.
- **Battery Life:** Aim for at least 60 minutes of runtime. Models with 90+ minutes are ideal, and a lithium-ion battery typically offers better performance and longevity. A quick-charge feature or the ability to use it while plugged in is a huge bonus.

Choosing the right trimmer requires a methodical approach to match the tool to your specific needs. Follow these steps to make the best choice.

2.  **Set Your Budget:** Decide what you're willing to spend. This will help you narrow down your options. Remember that a higher price often correlates with a more powerful motor, better build quality, and longer-lasting blades.
