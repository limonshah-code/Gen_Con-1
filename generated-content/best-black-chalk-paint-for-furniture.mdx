## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Black Chalk Paint For Furniture

Choosing the right black chalk paint involves more than just picking a color. To ensure your project is a success, consider these key factors before making a purchase.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, think about what you are painting. Is it a high-traffic item like a coffee table, or a decorative piece like a picture frame? For furniture that will see a lot of wear and tear, you need a highly durable paint or one that works well with a protective topcoat. Also, check the paint's compatibility with the material of your furniture—wood, metal, laminate, or glass. Some paints are more versatile than others. The size of your project also matters; a small 4 oz jar is great for crafts, but you'll need a 30 oz can or more for a large dresser.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($10-$40 ranges)
Black chalk paint comes in a wide range of prices. A small craft-sized jar can cost around $10, while a larger quart-sized can for furniture can range from $25 to $40. Value packs or kits that include brushes can sometimes offer a better deal per ounce. While it might be tempting to go for the cheapest option, remember that higher-quality paints often provide better coverage, meaning you'll use less paint overall.
